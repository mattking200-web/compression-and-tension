Meet the Two Forces! 💪

🤜🤛

Compression

Compression is a squeezing force. It pushes materials together. Think of squishing a sponge with both hands!

SQUEEZES & PUSHES
↔️

Tension

Tension is a pulling force. It stretches materials apart. Think of pulling both ends of an elastic band!
